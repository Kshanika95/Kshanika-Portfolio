Who I am

The researcher who stayed in the room.

I'm a Senior UX Researcher with 6+ years of experience helping product teams truly understand the people they build for. Somewhere along the way I got tired of handing off insights and watching the "so what?" get lost in translation — so I started designing too.

I care deeply about making research feel accessible to everyone on the team, building genuine trust with participants, and creating experiences that earn their place in someone's day.

Outside of work you'll find me painting, haunting art museums, obsessing over type pairings, and hunting the perfect coffee shop to read in. I bring that same curiosity — and stubbornly high standards — to everything I make.
